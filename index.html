Welcome

Welcome to the Computational Biology Lab at the University of Toronto's Department of Computer Science. The Computer Science Department is a member of the Collaborative Graduate Program in Genome Biology and Bioinfromatics.

About

The researchers in the group are interested in the development of novel computational approaches for analysis, storage, and modeling of biological data. The group combines faculty members with background in diverse areas of computer science, including algorithms, databases, and machine learning who apply their methodologies to disparate biological problems such as gene function characterization, genome analysis, understanding of protein structure, and computational medicine.
